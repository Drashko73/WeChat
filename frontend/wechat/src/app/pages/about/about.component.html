Our Story

WeChat began with a simple idea: to create a communication platform that brings people closer together, no matter where they are in the world. Founded in 2025, our team set out to build a messaging app that prioritizes user experience, security, and real-time connectivity.

We believe that communication should be effortless, secure, and enjoyable - and that's exactly what we've built.

Our Mission

At WeChat, we're on a mission to make digital communication as natural and engaging as face-to-face conversations. We're committed to continuously improving our platform to provide the best possible experience for our users.
